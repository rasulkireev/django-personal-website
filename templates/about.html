About

I started this website as a place to document everything I learn while on my path to become a Data Scientist and an Indie maker and generally a good person. I am trying to make a habit of writing about everything I learn.

My current goal is to find an entry level job in the Data Science field. I also want to make a useful product/app that would be useful to people but would also create an additional independent income stream, to support my family, financially.
